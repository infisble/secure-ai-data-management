# EU AI/Data Release Checklist

- [ ] AI Act role is documented: provider, deployer, importer, distributor, or product manufacturer.
- [ ] Intended purpose is documented.
- [ ] Risk classification is documented.
- [ ] High-risk triggers are reviewed, including credit, employment, education, essential services, biometrics, law enforcement, migration, justice, and critical infrastructure.
- [ ] GDPR lawful basis is documented.
- [ ] DPIA decision is documented; DPIA completed when required.
- [ ] Personal data minimization is implemented.
- [ ] Prompt, trace, embedding, and audit retention periods are documented.
- [ ] Data subject request handling is supported where applicable.
- [ ] Human oversight workflow is implemented for legal or similarly significant effects.
- [ ] User AI disclosure is implemented where transparency duties apply.
- [ ] Serious incident and cybersecurity incident routes are documented.
- [ ] Model/provider data processing terms are reviewed.
- [ ] Cross-border transfer and data residency constraints are reviewed.
