# Human Approval Policy Template

Human approval is mandatory when an agent proposes:

- Money movement.
- Account/card/customer-impacting action.
- External customer communication in regulated contexts.
- Permission changes.
- Deletion or export of confidential/restricted data.
- Any action based on untrusted external content.

Approval record must include:

- Approver identity.
- Requesting actor identity.
- Agent ID and version.
- Policy version.
- Exact action and parameters.
- Data sources used.
- Risk flags.
- Timestamp.
- Final decision.
