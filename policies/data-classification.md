# Data Classification Policy Template

## Public

Information approved for public release.

Allowed in prompts: yes.

## Internal

Company information that is not public but does not identify customers, employees, or sensitive systems.

Allowed in prompts: only for approved enterprise model deployments.

## Confidential

Customer records, employee records, contracts, non-public financial information, operational reports, and regulated business data.

Allowed in prompts: only after purpose limitation, access check, minimization, and redaction where possible.

## Restricted

Credentials, authentication secrets, full card numbers, full account numbers, government IDs, full raw KYC packages, private keys, session tokens, and security bypass details.

Allowed in prompts: no by default. Exceptions require documented security approval, contractual approval, and compensating controls.
